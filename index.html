The 7th International Workshop on Internet of Everything and Machine Learning Applications (IOEMLA-2025)

In conjunction with The 39th International Conference on Advanced Information Networking and Applications (AINA-2025)

Open University of Catalonia, Barcelona, Spain
April 9 to April 11, 2025

Scope

The 7th International Workshop on Internet of Everything and Machine Learning Applications (IOEMLA-2025) aims to explore the convergence of machine learning, Internet of Things (IoT), and emerging technologies such as the metaverse to create innovative solutions and enhance technological interoperability. This workshop seeks to highlight how machine learning can optimize IoT systems, improving efficiency and functionality across various industries including healthcare, manufacturing, and urban planning. By integrating advanced machine learning algorithms with robust IoT networks, the workshop will address challenges related to data complexity, system security, and scalability. Participants will engage in discussions on ethical implications, ensuring technology advancements align with societal values and privacy concerns. The event will serve as a platform for researchers, technologists, and industry professionals to exchange knowledge, fostering collaborations that propel the frontiers of technology in real-world applications.

Topics of Interest

  • Advanced Machine Learning Algorithms for IoT
  • Security and Privacy in IoT Networks
  • Metaverse Applications in Real-world Scenarios
  • Edge Computing and its Integration with IoT
  • AI-driven Automation in Smart Cities
  • Sustainable IoT Systems for Environmental Monitoring
  • Real-time Data Processing and Analytics in IoT
  • Blockchain Solutions for Enhanced IoT Security
  • Interoperability Standards for IoT Devices
  • Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) in Education IoT
